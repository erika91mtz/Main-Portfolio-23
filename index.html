Yoga Trapeze

Why try the Yoga Trapeze? Instant Inversion Therapy -Deeper Yoga Backbends, Core & Upper Body Strengthening.
Yoga trapeze can improve flexibility and posture. The twists and stretches in yoga can help open up tight muscles that may have become stiff over time, causing the body to feel more relaxed. Students are often attracted to the Yoga Trapeze for spinal traction and passive backbends, but very quickly they learn that the functional pulling and grip strength offered compliments a mat-based yoga practice to create a full-body fitness routine.

Hiking

Lets go for a hike! The activity of going for long walks, especially in the country or woods.
Hiking is a weight-bearing exercise, which builds muscle mass, helps manage your weight and helps prevent osteoporosis. In addition to being a good form of exercise, hiking is a joint-friendly form of exercise. Walking through nature comes with other health benefits like lowering your blood pressure, improving sleep and reducing depression and anxiety. For an added bonus, being outside in the sunshine provides the body with vitamin D. Lets go hiking today.
